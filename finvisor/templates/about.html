About Page

The FinVisor web app is a financial awareness tool created by Syed Rizvi. The idea originally started with a group of four programmers at the HackTX 2019 hackathon, after which Syed continued working on the web app version of the program.

The aim of this app is to make people more aware of their financial standing by giving them a convinient place to input expenses and income. Future plans for the app include creating a system of financial advising on the app, to give users further guidance and help them make more informed financial decisions.
